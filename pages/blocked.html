Let's Get Back to Focus!

It looks like you were about to visit a site that might interrupt your flow. No worries, it happens!

Here are a few ideas to help you refocus and make the most of your time:

Quick Tips for Refocusing:

  • Take a Short Break: Stand up, stretch, or grab a glass of water.
  • Review Your Goals: Briefly look at your to-do list for today.
  • The Pomodoro Technique: Work in focused 25-minute intervals.
  • Minimize Distractions: Close other unnecessary tabs or apps.
  • One Task at a Time: Concentrate on completing a single task before moving on.
